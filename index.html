CBD Bioservices

About

CBD Bio is an LLC formed by directors and executives of Thar Process, Inc and O.penVAPE, Inc. Thar Process is a global leader in supercritical fluid technology and equipment used for natural product extraction and separation. Supercritical fluid extraction is used for a wide variety of crops including spices, seeds, fruits, nuts, herbs, vegetables, algae, and other plant materials. Extracted materials include nutraceuticals, bioactive and pharmaceutical ingredients, natural products and biochemicals.
